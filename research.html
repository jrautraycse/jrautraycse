Research

Grants

  • Collaboration with ESCORTS Limited

    • Our team got the seed funding of USD 2000 for the proposed model of an autonomous weeder in collaboration with the research team of ESCORTS Limited. The system would use convolutional neural networks for achieving an efficient image classification system that would classify plants from unwanted weed. This would prove quintessential for the agriculture sector as it could possibly replace manual and chemical weeding techniques.

    • Led the development team to implement a computer vision assisted model to classify weed and cabbages using Tensorflow.

    • Designed and developed an table-top model to simulate the autonomous weeding process.

  • TEQIP II (Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme (TEQIP II)

    • Seed funded project Doot 1.0 under the guidance of Mrs. Swarnalata Pati, the then HOD, Dept. Of CSE, College of Engineering and Technology, Bhubaneswar. The project envisaged the idea of establishing a communication model for the hearing and speech impaired individuals. The limitations of the flex sensors and data glove assisted technology was established and an improved model was proposed using leap motion controller.

    • The single handed alphabet system under the Indian Sign language system was adopted as the scope of the project. Different supervised learning approaches were used and a differential set division classification was proposed to increase the efficiency and robustness of the system.
